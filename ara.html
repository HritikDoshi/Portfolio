MICRO-CONTROLLERS (Arduino/ Raspberry Pi/ Atmega) Projects

A microcontroller is a small and low-cost microcomputer, which is designed to perform the specific tasks of embedded systems. Microcontrollers are used in automatically controlled products and devices, such as automobile engine control systems, implantable medical devices, remote controls, appliances and other embedded systems. By reducing the size and cost, microcontrollers make it economical to digitally control even more devices and processes.

In this section of projects, i will be using microcontrollers like Arduino Uno, Raspberry Pi and also Atmega 2560 for as the main controlling units showcasing my projects. Few projects are implemented practically and few are implemented on virtual/online softwares either on Tinkercad or Proteus 8 Demonstration.

Home Gardening (IoT)

Home Gardening (IoT)

New to gardening? Don't know which corner of your house is more lite by the sun during day-time while you out for work?
Don't worry! This IoT project was created for people like you. It is a small gadget, comprised with Iot module which uses LDR(Light dependednt Resistor). How this gadget work is, you need to site it in any area of your house and the LDR will collect the readings of how much that area is lite my the sun and will upload this data (i.e. readings) to the cloud storage which you can access it remotely from any place through an App and keep a check on the data including the time and decide where you should start gardening in your house when you are outdoor.
